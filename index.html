About

Thanks for stopping by! I'm a senior at Santa Clara University studying Computer Science and Mathematics. Here, I'm a Teaching Assistant for introductory Computer Science courses. I'm also going to be a Teaching Assistant for Santa Clara Laptop Orchestra this winter quarter (2020)

I'm currently seeking full-time Software Engineering opportunities starting June 2020. I'm interested in building technology for social benefit.

I spent most of my summer overhauling an open-source project to build an e-commerce website for a container security startup in stealth mode. I learned the importance of cloud infrastructure and scalability. I also learned how I learn -- under minimal supervision with the freedom to explore and resources to approach once I've attempted to solve the problem at hand.

Earlier this year, I created and conducted a sound design workshop at R F Elementary School. I conducted the workshop with no music equipment -- just a laptop. I'm hoping to scale the workshop to multiple schools now! Last year, I built a Little Free Library with a team of two for Rocketship Mosaic Elementary School -- they didn't have a library before this.

Other that that, I have been immersed in the computer music space. As a Computer Musician for the Santa Clara Laptop Orchestra, I live-code and perform algorithmic music compositions. Our most recent gigs were at Stanford's CCRMA and Dolby Headquarters!
